How can you help?

Donation for Food and Veterinary Bills

Donate to help us feed and "trap, spray/neuter, and release" the hundreds of cats and kittens in the colonies that we care for every day.

Here's how your gift can help:

  • 1 Dollar: Feeds a cat for 2 days
  • 5 Dollars: Feeds a dog for 3 days
  • 10 Dollars: Deworming one dog or two cats
  • 20 Dollars: Microchip one animal
  • 50 Dollars: Spay or neuter one small dog
  • 100 Dollars: Flea medication for 6 cats or dogs

No amount is too small when it comes to helpless animals. You can donate with a credit card, and the money will go into saving lives! Thank you!

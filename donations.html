How can you help?

Donation for Food and Veterinary Bills

Donate to help us feed and "trap, spray/neuter, and release" the hundreds of cats and kittens in the colonies that we care for every day.

Here's how your gift can help:

  • $1 : Feeds a cat for 2 days
  • $5 : Feeds a dog for 3 days
  • $10 : Deworming one dog or two cats
  • $20 : Microchip one animal
  • $50 : Spay or neuter one small dog
  • $100 : Flea medication for 6 cats or dogs

No amount is too small when it comes to helpless animals. Thank you!
